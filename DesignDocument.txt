Syndicate Zero:

Turn based game
    - Each player has a hand of cards
    - Each player has a Deck
    - They play cards based on the mana they have that turn [generally this is the turn number]
    - Players start with 10 HP on their main based
    - Cards can move up to their speed units away
    - Cards can attack other cards
    - Cards can attack Players
    - Some cards have ranged attacks
    - When cards attack other cards, if they are in range, they do damage
        - If the card lives, then it will also deal its damage to the attacking Card
    - Cards can be healed
    - Cards can be buffed
    - Cards can be debuffed
    - Cards can be targeted

Semi-linear randomly generated map.
    - Similar to pegglin and encyption
